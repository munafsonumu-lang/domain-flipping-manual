## 🎬 BUYER CATEGORIES & PLATFORMS

### **Category 1: Content Creators (YouTube, Blogs)**
**Where to find:** YouTube, Medium, Substack  
**Why they need domains:** Personal branding, SEO, link in bio authority  
**Platform priority:** YouTube DM > Instagram DM > Email > LinkedIn

**Where to find:** Instagram hashtag search, niche communities

**Where to find:** Google Maps, local directories  
**Why they need domains:** Professional image, local SEO, customer trust  
**Platform priority:** WhatsApp > Phone > Facebook > In-person  
**Response rate:** 10-18%

### **Category 4: Online Coaches**
**Where to find:** LinkedIn, Facebook groups, Instagram  
**Why they need domains:** Email credibility, client trust, authority  
**Platform priority:** LinkedIn DM > Email > Instagram DM > WhatsApp  
**Response rate:** 12-20%

### **Category 5: E-commerce Sellers**
**Where to find:** Amazon, Flipkart, seller forums  
**Why they need domains:** Brand diversification, repeat customers  
**Platform priority:** Email > WhatsApp > LinkedIn > Facebook  
**Response rate:** 15-22%

✅ Younger audience | ✅ Visual context | ✅ Easy to reference content  
**Best for:** Creators, influencers  
**Response time:** 1-4 hours

### **Platform 3: LinkedIn Messages** (MEDIUM CONVERSION)
✅ Business-focused | ✅ Professional tone | ✅ B2B credibility  
**Best for:** Consultants, coaches, B2B  
**Response time:** 2-6 hours

### **Platform 4: Email** (LOWER CONVERSION)
⚠️ Slower response | ⚠️ Gets lost in inbox

**Response time:** 1-3 days

## 🎯 OUTREACH METHODOLOGY

### **The Core Approach**

**Step 1: Research Phase (2 minutes per prospect)**
- Find target person/business
- Verify they need domain
- Check if they have existing domain
- Note their platform presence
- Identify best contact method

**Step 2: Personalization Phase (2 minutes per message)**
- Use their name
- Reference their specific work/content
- Mention specific benefit for THEM
- Show you know who they are

**Step 3: Value Proposition Phase**
- Explain why they need domain
- Show specific benefit for their situation
- Make it about THEM, not you
- Create urgency if applicable
- Clear next step

**Step 4: Follow-Up Phase**
- Day 2: Light reminder
- Day 4: Value-add message
- Day 6: Final offer/scarcity
- After day 6: Move to next prospect

**Step 5: Closing Phase**
- Answer objections
- Negotiate if needed
- Register domain
- Complete transaction
- Collect payment

## 🎓 MESSAGING BEST PRACTICES

### **What WORKS:**
✅ Personalize with name  
✅ Reference their specific content  
✅ Focus on THEIR benefit  
✅ Keep under 100 words  
✅ Use 2-3 emojis (breaks text)  
✅ Clear call to action  
✅ Follow-up strategy  

### **What DOESN'T WORK:**
❌ Generic "Hi there"  
❌ Mass copy-paste  
❌ Focus on YOUR product  
❌ No personalization  
❌ Long paragraphs  
❌ No emojis  
❌ Unclear next step
