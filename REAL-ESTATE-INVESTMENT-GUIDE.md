## 📋 PROPERTY BUYING CHECKLIST

Before buying ANY property, verify:

```
LEGAL VERIFICATION:
☐ Property deed clear (no disputes)
☐ All taxes paid
☐ No pending litigation
☐ Title insurance available
☐ Municipal approval in place

STRUCTURAL VERIFICATION:
☐ Age of building (avoid >30 years)
☐ Building maintenance (good condition)
☐ Water & electricity working
☐ No water seepage/structural issues

FINANCIAL VERIFICATION:
☐ Cost reasonable for area
☐ Comparable properties checked
☐ Registration charges clear
☐ Loan approval confirmed

RENTAL VERIFICATION:
☐ Area has high rental demand
☐ Similar properties rent for ₹40-50k
☐ Occupancy rate >90%
☐ Market appreciation likely (8-10%/year)
```
