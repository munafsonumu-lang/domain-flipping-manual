# ✅ 4-POINT DOMAIN VERIFICATION CHECKLIST

## NEVER Buy a Domain Without Verifying ALL 4 Points

This checklist eliminates 95% of risky domains and protects your capital.

**Time per domain: 5-10 minutes**

---

## 🔍 POINT 1: WHOIS & AVAILABILITY CHECK (2 minutes)

### **Step 1: Check WHOIS Registry**

**Go to:** https://www.whois.com/whois/

1. Enter domain name: `[example].in`
2. Look for:
   - ✅ **Available** = Good to buy
   - ❌ **Registered** = Skip this domain
   - ❌ **Premium** = Too expensive

**What to note:**
- Current registrar (if registered)
- Expiration date (if registered)
- Registration date (if very old)

---

### **Step 2: Check Registrar Price**

**Go to:** https://www.godaddy.in/

1. Search the domain: `[example].in`
2. Check price:
   - ✅ Under ₹500 = Great buy
   - ✅ ₹500-1,000 = Good buy
   - ⚠️ ₹1,000+ = Only if strong potential buyer

**Rule:** Buy price should be 25-40% of potential selling price

---

## 🏢 POINT 2: TRADEMARK & BRAND CHECK (2 minutes)

### **Check if Domain Violates Trademark**

**Go to:** https://ipindia.gov.in/

1. Click "Search Trademarks"
2. Search for the domain name
3. Look for exact/similar trademarks

**Red flags:**
- ❌ Exact match trademark exists = DON'T BUY
- ❌ Similar brand registered = DON'T BUY
- ❌ Trademarked company name = DON'T BUY
- ✅ No trademark match = SAFE TO BUY

**Example:**
- ❌ "Nike.in" = Trademarked, don't buy
- ✅ "NikeCoaching.in" = Not trademarked, safe to buy

---

### **Also Check Google Trademark**

1. Go to Google
2. Search: `"[domain]" trademark`
3. See if major brand is associated

---

## 🌐 POINT 3: HISTORICAL CONTENT CHECK (3 minutes)

### **Check if Domain Has Bad History**

**Go to:** https://web.archive.org/

1. Enter domain name: `[example].in`
2. Click "Browse History"
3. Look for:
   - ✅ **No history** = Clean domain
   - ⚠️ **Old legitimate content** = Okay
   - ❌ **Spam/adult/illegal content** = DON'T BUY
   - ❌ **Multiple owners** = Skip
   - ❌ **Phishing/scam history** = NEVER BUY

**What to look for:**
- What was the site about?
- When was it last active?
- Does it have spam characteristics?

**Example clean domain:**
- Old blog about fitness (legitimate)
- No content for 5+ years (safe)
- No spam indicators (good)

**Example bad domain:**
- Adult content history (avoid)
- Phishing site (NEVER)
- Spam for 10 years (avoid)

---

### **Also Google the Domain**

1. Go to Google
2. Search: `site:[domain]`
3. See what appears
4. Search: `[domain] spam` or `[domain] scam`

---

## 💰 POINT 4: BUYER POTENTIAL CHECK (2-3 minutes)

### **This is the MOST IMPORTANT check**

Before buying ANY domain, ask:

**Question 1: WHO would buy this domain?**
- Be specific (not "anyone")
- Name the exact type of buyer
- Example: "YouTube fitness creators with 10k-100k subs"

**Question 2: HOW MANY such buyers exist?**
- Estimate: 10? 100? 1,000?
- Rule: Need at least 50+ potential buyers
- Example: "100+ fitness creators in India want custom domains"

**Question 3: WHAT would they PAY?**
- Research on Namecheap.com "recently sold"
- Check similar domain sales
- Rule: Buyers should pay 3-5x your cost
- Example: "Buy at ₹500, sell at ₹2,000-3,000"

**Question 4: HOW EASY to find these buyers?**
- Can you find them on Instagram? ✅
- Can you find them on YouTube? ✅
- Can you find them on Google Maps? ✅
- Are they hidden/hard to reach? ❌

## ✅ THE VERIFICATION FORMULA

**Before buying any domain, answer these 4 questions:**

```
POINT 1: AVAILABILITY & PRICE
Q: Is domain available and under ₹500?
A: [YES/NO]

POINT 2: TRADEMARK CLEAR
Q: Does it have trademark conflicts?
A: [NONE/MINOR/MAJOR]
(Only proceed if NONE)

POINT 3: CLEAN HISTORY
Q: Does it have spam/bad history?
A: [CLEAN/QUESTIONABLE/BAD]
(Only proceed if CLEAN)

POINT 4: BUYER POTENTIAL
Q: Can I sell for 3-5x cost?
A: [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW]
(Only proceed if HIGH/MEDIUM)

FINAL DECISION: [BUY/SKIP]
```

## 🚨 RED FLAGS (SKIP THESE DOMAINS)

❌ **Any trademark conflicts** - Never worth it
❌ **Adult/gambling content history** - Brand damage
❌ **Spam history** - Search engines penalize
❌ **Phishing/scam history** - Legal risk
❌ **No clear buyer pool** - Can't sell
❌ **Oversaturated niche** - 10,000 competitors
❌ **Regional/local only** - Limited buyer pool
❌ **Requires heavy marketing** - Hard to sell quick

---

## 🟢 GREEN FLAGS (BUY THESE DOMAINS)

✅ **Clear trademark check** - No legal issues
✅ **Clean history** - Good for brand
✅ **Specific niche with buyers** - Easy to sell
✅ **Under ₹500 cost** - Good margin
✅ **3-5x resale potential** - Profitable
✅ **Buyer pool: 50-1,000+ people** - Good market
✅ **Can find buyers easily** - Instagram/YouTube
✅ **Can sell within 2-4 weeks** - Quick turnaround
